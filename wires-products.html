Wires and Cables Products

XHHW-2 Aluminium Conductor :-

This specification applies to a single-core aluminum conductor building wire, insulated with a high-heat and water-resistant XLPE compound. Designed for general-purpose use, it is suitable for lighting and power circuits operating at up to 600 volts in residential, commercial, and industrial applications. The wire can be installed in wet or dry locations, with a maximum conductor operating temperature of 90°C.

URD Single Aluminum Conductor :-

The Aluminum 600V URD cable is engineered for secondary distribution circuits and is suitable for installation in ducts or direct burial applications. It has a voltage rating of 600 volts and is designed for a maximum operating temperature of 90°C in both wet and dry locations.

URD Duplex Aluminum Conductor :-

The Aluminum 600V URD cable is intended for secondary distribution circuits and can be installed in ducts or direct burial applications. It is rated for 600 volts and has a maximum operating temperature of 90°C in both wet and dry conditions. Additionally, this cable can be supplied pre-pulled into duct upon request.

URD Triplex Aluminum Conductor :-

The Aluminum 600V URD cable is specifically designed for secondary distribution circuits and is suitable for installation in ducts or for direct burial. It has a voltage rating of 600 volts, with a maximum operating temperature of 90°C in both wet and dry environments. For added convenience, this cable can also be supplied pre-pulled into a duct if required.

URD Quadruplex Aluminum Conductor :-

The Aluminum 600V URD cable is designed for secondary distribution circuits and is suitable for installation in ducts or direct burial applications. It has a voltage rating of 600 volts and can operate at temperatures up to 90°C in both wet and dry conditions. For convenience, this cable can be provided pre-pulled into duct.

SER Service Entrance Cable :-

Constructed with compact AA-8000 series aluminum alloy conductors, this cable features individually color-coded XHHW-2 insulated conductors, which are cabled together with an uninsulated bare ground wire. A flame-retardant, fiberglass-reinforced binder tape holds the assembly together, which is then covered with a sunlight-resistant (SUN RES marked), flame-retardant gray polyvinyl chloride (PVC) jacket. Phase conductors are identified by colored stripes on the insulation: • 3-conductor: black, black with red stripe • 4-conductor: black, black with white stripe, black with red stripe • 5-conductor: black, black with white stripe, black with red stripe, black with blue stripe

SEU Service Entrance Cable :-

Constructed with compact AA-8000 series aluminum alloy conductors, this cable features individual conductors with color-coded XHHW-2 insulation. Two insulated phase conductors are surrounded by a concentric neutral wire. The assembly is secured with a flame-retardant, fiberglass-reinforced binder tape and finished with a gray, sunlight-resistant (SUN RES marked), flame-retardant polyvinyl chloride (PVC) outer jacket.

Mobile Home Feeder Cable :-

Mobile Home Feeder Cable is approved for direct burial and is suitable for installation in raceways and underground applications. It can be directly buried in earth, with conductor temperatures not exceeding 90°C. The cable has a voltage rating of 600 volts.
