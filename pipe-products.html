Pipe & Conduit Products

Galvanized Rigid Metal Conduits (GRMC) :-

Steel pipes protected with a galvanizing process, ideal for plumbing, tubing, gas, and sewage applications. Offered in standard dimensions as well as customized sizes, widths, and thicknesses to meet specific requirements.

Black Pipes :-

Steel and iron pipes designed for water supply and flammable gas applications. These pipes offer excellent heat and corrosion resistance and are available in round, rectangular, and square profiles.

Electrical Metallic Tubing (EMT) :-

The exterior and interior of the EMT are protected with a zinc coating applied through an in-line hot-dip galvanizing process. Additionally, the exterior features a clear anti-corrosion coating or a zinc chromate coating, while the interior is treated with an organic zinc lubricant or a premium-grade organic epoxy resin for enhanced protection.

Hot Dip Galvanized Pipes :-

These pipes are coated with an extra layer of zinc by immersing the metal in a bath of molten zinc, providing excellent corrosion resistance. They can be easily welded and are well-suited for high-temperature applications.

Electrical Intermediate Metal Conduit (EIMC) :-

Our IMC is about one-third lighter than rigid metal conduits, helping reduce costs and boost productivity. It can be used interchangeably with rigid conduits and is compatible with the same threads, couplings, and fittings.

Flexible GI Conduit :-

Designed for applications requiring flexibility and strength, this galvanized iron conduit offers excellent mechanical protection for electrical wiring. Its corrugated design ensures durability while allowing easy bending and installation in confined spaces.

ERW Galvanized Pipes :-

Electric Resistance Welded Galvanized Pipes are manufactured from high-quality steel strips using advanced welding techniques. These pipes are zinc-coated for enhanced corrosion resistance, making them ideal for use in structural, water supply, and industrial applications.
